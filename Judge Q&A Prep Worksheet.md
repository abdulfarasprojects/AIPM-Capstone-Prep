## How to use this worksheet

- A 15-second direct answer

If you fill this out fully, you will be able to answer most judge questions without improvising.

## 16. Best Answer Formula

When you are on the spot, use this order:

`Direct answer -> evidence -> tradeoff -> next step`

Example:

`We are starting with [segment] because [reason]. We saw that in [evidence]. The tradeoff is [constraint]. The next step is [pilot, metric, or validation move].`
